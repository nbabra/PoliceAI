Preparing for the Future

Hayes Valley/Tenderloin/North of Market (94102), Polk/Russian Hill (94109), and Inner Mission/Bernal Heights (94110) saw the greatest increase in dispatch calls. Medical Assistance was the greatest type of incident in these areas. Greater ambulance services in these areas would aid the increase of these types of calls, especially in the Tenderloin area, which is in between the other two areas.
